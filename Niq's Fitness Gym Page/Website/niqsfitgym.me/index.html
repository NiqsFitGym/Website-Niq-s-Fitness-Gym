FAQ

We answer your questions here!

Question 01: Boss, what if I don't want to sign-up for a membership yet?

Ok lang yan po! If you wish to try out Niq's Fitness Gym now, you can have a walk-in session with unlimited hours from the time you sign-in to the time you sign-out. Till then, we are eager for your decision to be a part of our gym community!

Question 02: Boss, is there a trainer or coach that will guide me when I start out?

Yes, we have a coach that will offer advice and training from 6 PM - 10 PM. If you wish to learn about the fees, it is PHP 300 per session and PHP 4,000 for a monthly training service. Serbisyo yan ni Coach John!

Question 03: What are your operating hours, boss?

Niq's Fitness Gym is open for your gains from 8 AM - 10 PM, 7 days a week.

Question 04: Boss, where are you located?

Niq's Fitness Gym is located at 9062 J.P Rizal Bagong Silangan in front of Meadow Heights Subdivision. Look for stairs leading up to the 2nd floor, and you will find Niq's Fitness Gym there.

Question 05: Boss, is it squeaky clean there?

Yes, our janitor cleans all amenities, facilities, and equipment every morning. We also encourage members to clean up after themselves. Additionally, we have exhaust fans to get rid of any sweaty smell.
